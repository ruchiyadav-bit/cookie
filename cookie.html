Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and understand website traffic.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve functionality.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve site performance, understand visitor activity, remember settings, and support a better browsing experience.

3. Types of Cookies We May Use

We may use essential cookies for website functionality, analytics cookies to understand traffic, and preference cookies to remember basic user choices.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services such as analytics or advertising tools that help us measure performance and improve our services.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can accept or decline cookies through our website notice where available. You can also control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
